About Us

The Miami Heat (in Spanish, Miami Heat) are an NBA basketball team based in Miami, Florida. He plays his home games at the American Airlines Arena and is part of the Southeast Division of the NBA's Eastern Conference.

The team was founded in 1988 thanks to the expansion of the NBA in 1987. It has three NBA titles, obtained in the 2005-06 season by beating the Dallas Mavericks 4-2, in the 2011-12 defeating Oklahoma City Thunder 4-1 in the series, in addition to the most recent one obtained in 2012-13 by defeating the San Antonio Spurs in a maximum of 7 games. In addition to the three rings, the Heat have earned 11 division titles, achieved in the franchise's three most glorious eras: 1997-2000, 2005-2007 and 2011-2014.

League stars such as Alonzo Mourning, Glen Rice, Tim Hardaway, Gary Payton, Shaquille O'Neal, Dwyane Wade, Chris Bosh, LeBron James and Jimmy Butler have played in their ranks.
